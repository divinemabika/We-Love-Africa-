Educating Africa!

BUILD, STRENGTHEN AND EMPOWER!

Educating Africa and what it aims to do is give Africans the best possible education whether it is in a formal setting or an informal setting. I have realised that within trying to educate people from Africa through development education and global learning, they are being taught a lot of things that have derived from the countries in the Global North. They will learn about their history, read books from European writers, learning about the conditions in their country and an example of this is Chimanda Adiche The danger of a single story. The Nigerian author, in her TED talk refers to her childhood education, where she learnt much more about snow, raining and life in the UK through English literature than she did about the history and culture of her own country. It is not therefore surprising that to this day the influence of the UK education system can be seen in countries as diverse as Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, India and Pakistan.
